What exactly is WatchR

WatchR is a completely new way to buy and sell luxury watches online. We help buyers and sellers in a trusted, simple and secure environment. All WatchR watches must pass our comprehensive inspection process and become WatchR-Certified. They are then listed and made available to buyers. We’ve cut out the costs of a traditional dealership – no showy showrooms and no expensive salespeople. Because of that, we can pass along more value to our customers. Sellers are guaranteed payment in 30 days. And if you change your mind, you have ten days to return your watch for a full refund, according to our terms and conditions.
