Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to sell my watch on WatchR?

There’s a great team of highly energised WatchRs working around the clock to find a buyer who loves your watch as much as you do.
While many factors affect when a watch sells, we try our best to make sure the sale happens at the soonest. On average our watches sell in 30 days.

What happens at a WatchR inspection? Can you do it at my home? And do I need to be there?

WatchR inspection is a 30 minutes comprehensive assessment of your watch.
We want to make the process as easy and hassle-free as possible, so we’ll come to you and inspect your watch wherever it is. You can feel free to attend as much or as little of the inspection as you please.
Our trained inspectors examine the aesthetic conditions, the mechanism, the box, the papers and cross check the reference numbers. If the inspection is a pass, they also take professional photos of your timepiece.

Who are the WatchR inspectors, anyway?

Just like we hand-pick all of our watches, we do the same for our amazing inspectors. All WatchR inspectors are certified, have formal technical training from accredited institutions and many years of experience in the field. They also undergo extensive screening procedures before coming on board.

What happens after my watches passes the inspection?

Congratulations! We are honored to have your vehicle as part of the WatchR family.
After your watch passes its inspection, we'll ask for you to confirm by email or phone that you'd like to sell your watch online with WatchR. As soon as we find a buyer, we will contact you to pick up your watch and arrange the ownership transfer.

How do you price my watch?

We pride ourselves on our data driven approach. This means that we analyze pricing from a variety of sources and our own model to determine the true market value of your watch.
We're certain our offers are higher than any dealer will give you for your timepiece. In fact, if a dealership gives you a written non-contingent offer that's higher than ours, we'll beat it!

Are there any hidden fees?

No. WatchR is completely free for sellers.
How do we cover our costs, you ask? Simple. WatchR takes a small markup on the transaction between sellers and buyers, which shows up as a difference between the price we offer to sellers and the price buyers pay. That’s it.

What if I sell my watch, but the buyer returns it?

Once the buyer reserves your watch online, we keep the money in a secured account for 7 days. If, at the end of it, the buyer does not return the item, we will transfer the money immediately net of our commission.
If the buyer decides to return the item, WatchR proceed to another full check to make sure nothing has changed from the time of purchase. If the inspection is a pass, we will ship the watch back to you and refund the buyer. Otherwise, we reject the return and pay you immediately. For further information about returns, please check our Terms and Conditions.

What if I don’t have time to wait, but I still want to sell my watch?

Depending on the circumstances we may purchase your watch outright after the inspection.
